## Summary

While browsing products using the `problem_user` account, some product images do not match the corresponding product details.

---

## Preconditions

- Login as `problem_user`

---

## Steps to Reproduce

1. Login with `problem_user`.
2. Navigate to the Products page.
3. Observe the product images.

---

## Expected Result

Each product should display its correct image.

---

## Actual Result

Some products display incorrect or mismatched images.

---

## Reproducibility

Always (100%)
